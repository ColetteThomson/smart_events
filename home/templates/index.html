Welcome to SPMS - the SmartEvents Project Management System !

SPMS operates on your organisation's intranet, enabling the matching of available People resources to the business's active Projects.

SPMS also offers a Project Chat encouraging project members to add their Ideas or Suggestions on the organisation's different projects.

What can you do with SPMS ?

As a Project Manager:

CRUD*: own projects

R: other projects; all People resources

As an Administration People Owner:

CRUD*: Project Administration People

R: all projects; all Tech Support People

As a Tech Support People Owner:

CRUD*: Tech Support People

R: all projects; all Project Admin People

General SPMS users (project staff):

R: all Chat posts

C: add ideas or suggestions on all projects

* CRUD: Create, Read (view), Update, Delete
