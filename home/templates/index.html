Welcome to SPMS - the SmartEvents Project Management System !

SPMS operates on your organisation's intranet, enabling the matching of available People resources to the business's active Projects.

SPMS also offers a Project Chat encouraging project members to add their Ideas or Suggestions on the organisation's different projects.

What can you do with SPMS ?

you can create, read, update and delete information on your own projects; view information on other projects; and view all available Administration and Tech Support People project resources
you can create, read, update and delete information on all Administration project resources in SPMS - and view information on all projects
you can create, read, update and delete information on all Tech Support project resources in SPMS - and view information on all projects
can view and add their ideas or suggestions on the projects' Chat
