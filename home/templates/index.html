Welcome to SPMS - the SmartEvents Project Management System!

SPMS operates on your organisation's intranet, enabling personnel working on projects to match available People resources to the business's different Projects.

SPMS also offers a Project Chat encouraging project members to add their Ideas or Suggestions on the organisation's active projects.

What can you do with SPMS?

As a registered Project Manager: you can create, read, update and delete information on your own projects; view information on other projects; and view all available Administration and Tech Support People project resources
As a registered Administration People Owner: you can create, read, update and delete information on all Administration project resources in SPMS - and view information on all projects
As a registered Tech Support People Owner: you can create, read, update and delete information on all Tech Support project resources in SPMS - and view information on all projects
All SPMS users (both registered and general project personnel): can view and add their ideas or suggestions on the projects' Chat
