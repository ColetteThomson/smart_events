Welcome to the smartevents Workforce Management Platform ( WMP)

WMPoperates on your organisation's intranet, and is a tool used by smart project managers to select the right resource for the right task. Also available is a project Chat, encouraging project team members to add their ideas or suggestions on the organisation's active projects.
