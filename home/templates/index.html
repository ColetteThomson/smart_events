Welcome to SPMS - the SmartEvents Project Management System!

SPMS will help you match available people resources to your projects.

SPMS also offers a Project Chat which enables project staff to leave their Ideas or Suggestions on the various projects.

What can you do with SPMS?

As a Project Manager - you can create, read, update and delete information on your own projects
As a People Owner - you can create, read, update and delete information on all project people resources in SPMS
As a Site User - you can view and leave ideas/suggestions on the projects Chat
