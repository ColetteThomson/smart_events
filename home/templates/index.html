Welcome to the smartevents Workforce Management Platform ( WMP)

WMPoperates on your organisation's intranet, and is a tool used by smart project managers to select the right resource for the right task.

Also available is a project Chat, encouraging project team members to add their ideas or suggestions on the organisation's active projects.

What are the different WMProles ?

Project Manager
  • Permissions:
  • Create, Read, Update, Delete: for all your projects
  • Read-only: for other projects, and all people resources
Admin People Owner
  • Permissions:
  • Create, Read, Update, Delete: for all Admin resources
  • Read-only: for all projects, and all Tech Support resources

Tech Support Owner
  • Permissions:
  • Create, Read, Update, Delete: for all Tech Support Resources
  • Read-only: for all projects, and all Administration resources
General WMP users
  • Permissions:
  • Read-only: all Chat posts
  • Create: add ideas or suggestions on all projects
